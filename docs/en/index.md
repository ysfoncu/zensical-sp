# StudentPlacement Help & Onboarding Center

Welcome! This site walks you through the **StudentPlacement test scenarios** step by step,
with screenshots for every stage.

## How it works

1. **Enter your email** — it is used only to track your progress and collect
   your feedback. No password needed.
2. **Work through the scenarios** in the sidebar, following the steps in the
   real application as you read.
3. **Submit feedback at the bottom of each scenario** — tell us whether it was
   useful and leave an optional comment. Submitting feedback marks the
   scenario as completed.
4. Check **My Progress** at any time to see what you have finished.
